SERVING SMILES - January 24, 2025

The program was succesfully held by students of St. Matthew, and was able to show appreciation and give fun to some of the canteen workers of Marist School Marikina.

The event started, with the opening remarks led by the Community Extension Coordinator, Mrs. Chona Manalili. After that, each of the 14 canteen workers introduced themselves. It was quickly proceeded by parlor games such as Musical Chairs to lighten up the mood. Students and teachers joined the fun as well!

The class prepared food for the canteen workers, and intermission performances were done while the workers dine on pancit, pizza, and bibingka. After the event, the class handed out aprons to each worker as a token of appreciation and kindness for their resilience and hard work in serving students. Closing remarks were given by one of the canteen workers, and a certificate was given.
